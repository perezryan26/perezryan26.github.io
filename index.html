Discussion of design and sketch

Generalized and Health-Related Information

  • Personalized Calendar (Retrieves the calendar data from a calendar based CSV file)
  • News Feed (Retrieves the news data from a news based CSV file)
  • Clock (Uses the local time)
  • Weather (Retrieves the local weather with an API)
  • Weight Analysis (Scans the weight using a built in scale, and stores in a CSV file)

Advanced Features

  • Customizable Widget Layout (User can change the location of the widgets after pressing the edit button)
  • Customizable LED Light Source (User can change the color of the LED lights by pressing a color on the screen)

All of the details from above have been implemented into the processing project

My mirror was created using processing and simulates what my smart mirror would look like in real life.

The smart mirror is intended to function through a touch screen interface

Some things were hard to simulate in processing such as looking at your reflection through the program, and simulating real LED lights.
